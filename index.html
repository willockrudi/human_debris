/// the band

Who We Are

No management. No stylist. No deal. Human Debris has been playing the same dirty, loud, mean rock and roll since the early nineties — back when a four-track and a van was all you needed.

The music lives somewhere between a Black Sabbath riff and a Clutch groove, dragged through the mud of every bar floor we've played on the West Coast. If it doesn't feel like it cost something, we didn't do it right.

Three records. All self-released. All recorded analogue. Take it or leave it.
